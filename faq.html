Empowerment Hub FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions




  • What is Empowerment Hub?
    Empowerment Hub is a youth-focused organization dedicated to creating impactful opportunities through internships, marketing projects, and capacity-building programs.
  • What services does Empowerment Hub provide?

    We offer a wide range of services including ATL & BTL marketing campaigns, event management, volunteer management, branding, financial counseling, investment planning, and promoter/share management.

  • Who can join Empowerment Hub’s programs or internships?

    Any youth aged 16–30 with a passion for marketing, leadership, and entrepreneurship can apply. We encourage students, recent graduates, and early-career professionals to join.

  • Where is Empowerment Hub located?

    Our head office is located at Lokanthali-01, Bhaktapur, Nepal.

  • How can I contact Empowerment Hub?

    You can reach us on WhatsApp at 9862867087 or via our social media platforms.

  • What types of marketing campaigns do you manage?

    We manage both Above-the-Line (ATL) and Below-the-Line (BTL) campaigns, including digital promotion, street marketing, activations, and branding events.

  • Do you offer financial counseling and investment planning?

    Yes, we provide expert financial counseling, investment guidance, and promoter/share management to help youth make informed financial decisions.

  • What kind of events do you organize?

    We organize corporate events, youth engagement programs, brand activations, awareness campaigns, and community impact events.

  • Is Empowerment Hub a registered Company?

    Yes, Empowerment Hub is a legally registered and professionally managed youth organization based in Nepal.

  • How can I volunteer or become a promoter with Empowerment Hub?

    Simply contact us via WhatsApp or visit our office. We regularly post opportunities for volunteers, interns, and promoters on our official platforms.

  • Can I partner or sponsor with Empowerment Hub?

    Absolutely! We welcome partnerships with companies, NGOs, and institutions for joint projects, sponsorships, and CSR initiatives.

  • Do you collaborate with other companies or brands?

    Yes, we collaborate with various brands, startups, and institutions to co-create impactful campaigns and youth programs.
